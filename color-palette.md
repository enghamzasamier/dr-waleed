# Dr. Waleed Ghoniem - Brand Color Palette

This color palette is designed to convey **trust, professionalism, prestige, and academic authority**—perfect for an expert in accounting, auditing, and academia.

## 1. Primary Color: Deep Royal Navy Blue
*   **Hex:** `#0F172A` (or `#1E3A8A` for slightly brighter navy)
*   **RGB:** `15, 23, 42`
*   **Usage:** Primary backgrounds, main headers, primary buttons, logo background.
*   **Meaning:** Represents stability, intelligence, corporate trust, and academic rigor.

## 2. Secondary/Accent Color: Champagne Gold
*   **Hex:** `#C5A880` (or `#D97706` for a deeper amber gold)
*   **RGB:** `197, 168, 128`
*   **Usage:** Logo typography, hover effects, highlight text, subtle borders, call-to-action accents.
*   **Meaning:** Conveys prestige, high value, success, and premium quality (perfect for consulting and leadership).

## 3. Neutral Dark: Slate Grey
*   **Hex:** `#334155`
*   **RGB:** `51, 65, 85`
*   **Usage:** Body text, secondary headers, footer backgrounds.
*   **Meaning:** Offers high readability and a modern, sleek alternative to harsh pure black.

## 4. Neutral Light / Background: Soft Off-White (Alabaster)
*   **Hex:** `#F8FAFC`
*   **RGB:** `248, 250, 252`
*   **Usage:** Main page backgrounds, card backgrounds, negative space.
*   **Meaning:** Clean, approachable, minimalist, and easy on the eyes for reading long academic CVs.
