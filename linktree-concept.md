# The "Financial Ledger" Link Page Concept

For the links page (e.g., `waleedghoniem.com/links`), we are designing a customized "Linktree" alternative that visually represents Dr. Waleed's field of **Accounting, Auditing, and Academia**.

## Concept: "The Balance Sheet of Success" / "The Professional Ledger"

**1. Design Layout**
The page will be styled to look like a modern, clean, digital accounting ledger or a well-formatted balance sheet table. It will abandon the standard "bubble buttons" of Linktree for a more sophisticated row-based design.

**2. Categories (The Ledger Entries)**
*   **Assets (Professional Links)**
    *   *Ref 01:* Google Scholar Profile (Value: Infinite)
    *   *Ref 02:* LinkedIn Profile (Value: Networking)
    *   *Ref 03:* ResearchGate / Publications (Value: Intellectual)
*   **Equity (Contact & Booking)**
    *   *Ref 04:* Email Dr. Waleed (Direct Line)
    *   *Ref 05:* WhatsApp for Consulting (Direct Action)
    *   *Ref 06:* Book a Training Session (Inquiry Form)

**3. Visual Style**
*   **Background:** Soft Off-White (`#F8FAFC`) to resemble premium paper.
*   **Dividers:** Thin Slate Grey (`#334155`) "ledger lines" separating each link.
*   **Interactions:** Hovering over a link will trigger a subtle Champagne Gold (`#C5A880`) highlight sweeping across the row, mimicking the highlighting of a line item on an audit report.
*   **Typography:** Monospaced or clean serif fonts for the reference numbers (e.g., `01`, `02`) to emulate accounting reference codes.
